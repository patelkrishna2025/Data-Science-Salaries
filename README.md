# Data-Science-Salaries
The project analyzes global Data Science job salaries using real-world data from ai-jobs.net. It explores how job roles, experience, company size, employment type, and location influence pay trends, helping professionals and organizations understand key salary drivers and market patterns.

# Dataset Description
* work_year – Year the salary was paid
* experience_level – Entry / Mid / Senior / Executive
* employment_type – Full-time / Part-time / Contract / Freelance
* job_title – Job role
* salary – Salary in USD
* employee_residence – Country where employee lives
* company_location – Company’s country
* company_size – Small / Medium / Large
* job_type – Remote / Hybrid / Onsite

# Insights
* Salary prediction works reasonably well.
* Experience level is the strongest predictor.
* Company size and job type also influence salary.

# Conclusion
The Data Science job market offers lucrative opportunities, especially for senior and remote positions. Salary is influenced by multiple factors such as experience, employment type,
company size, and location. This analysis provides a deep insight into global Data Science career trends and can support both job seekers and employers in making data-driven decisions.
